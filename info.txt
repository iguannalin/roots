二， when i look at these <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Radical_140">radicals</a>, i am reminded of the history, and the culture that these radicals have sustained, and continue to radicalize; for me, it characterizes a primal human desire to grow with each other, to empathize, and to share a common ground to understand each other better.
